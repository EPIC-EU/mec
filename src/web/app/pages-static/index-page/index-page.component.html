EPIC: Improving Employability through Internationalization and Collaboration

OBJECTIVES

EPIC is an Erasmus+ strategic partnership project focusing on employability through internationalization and collaboration between industry and academia.

FOR COMPANIES

EPIC is a new effort from the European Commission (Erasmus+) to increase the employability through closer collaboration between students, academia and industry.

FOR STUDENTS

EPIC provides a flexible framework for international student projects in close collaboration with companies. A unique opportunity to work together on a real-life problem.

ACTIVITIES

Yearly activities for students, institutions, and companies to increase collaboration, hosted by the partners in various European cities.
